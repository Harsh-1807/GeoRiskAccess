Katraj, nestled on the outskirts of Pune, is a burgeoning hub with a blend of urban amenities and suburban charm. Its proximity to the IT and manufacturing sectors makes it an attractive residential destination. While it offers affordable housing options, traffic congestion and limited public transportation can be drawbacks. The availability of essential services, educational institutions, and healthcare facilities makes it a relatively livable area. However, the lack of recreational spaces and environmental concerns require attention to enhance its livability in the long run.
